About Us

Mangalore University Alumni Association (MAA)

The Mangala Alumni Association is a dedicated network for all post-graduates and researchers, fostering lifelong connections and championing the continued success of our alma mater. Our mission is to strengthen the bond between alumni and the institution through networking, events, and community-building initiatives.

We believe that every graduate has a unique story, and together, these stories form the incredible narrative of Mangalore University. This network is where you can find old friends, discover new opportunities, and give back to the community that helped shape you. We provide a platform for alumni to reconnect with former classmates, engage in valuable mentorship opportunities, and contribute to the vibrant future of Mangalore University. So welcome home. Please do join us and stay connected with the global Mangalagangothri family.

Our Mission

To foster lifelong relationships, mentorship, and collaboration among alumni while supporting the university's development and community impact.

Our Vision

To build a strong global alumni network that connects, inspires, and contributes to the continued growth of Mangalore University.
